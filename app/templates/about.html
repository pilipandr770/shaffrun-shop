Our Story

Voloskyi Saffron is a family-run business committed to cultivating the finest saffron threads with respect for the earth and our artisans. From seed selection to careful harvesting, each step reflects centuries of heritage.

We partner with chefs, retailers, and wellness enthusiasts across Europe to elevate their offerings with authentic saffron. Sustainability guides our farming practices, while innovation powers our digital experiences, making saffron knowledge accessible to everyone.

Whether you are experimenting with haute cuisine or crafting wellness blends, we are here to provide trusted guidance and premium saffron.
